Route Info

The Scenic ECR Drive

The East Coast Road (ECR) is one of India's most beautiful highways — a coastal ribbon running alongside the Bay of Bengal from Chennai to Pondicherry.

01
Ocean Views the Entire Way
The ECR hugs the coastline, offering breathtaking sea views, beach resorts, and fresh salt air throughout your journey.
02
UNESCO Heritage Stopover
Pass through Mahabalipuram — home to ancient rock-cut temples and the famous Shore Temple, just 60 km from Chennai.
03
Auroville & French Quarter Arrival
Visit Auroville's golden Matrimandir, then arrive in Pondicherry's charming French Quarter with colonial villas and a seafront promenade.
